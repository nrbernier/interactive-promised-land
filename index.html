Welcome

This map shows, in simplified form, which political entity controlled each part of the Israel/Palestine region and its neighbors from 1000 BCE to today.

Drag the time slider at the bottom (or press ▶ to animate). Click any colored territory on the map, or any event dot above the slider, to see a summary, an illustration, and links to sources.

Borders are simplified, approximate reconstructions for educational purposes. Ancient frontiers were fluid and are debated by historians; several modern boundaries and statuses are disputed. Summaries and images are loaded live from Wikipedia.
